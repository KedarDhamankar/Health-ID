DATA MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

It is a digital and decentralized blockchain based access management system which stores patient data, research cases etc. Here the patient data is stored in the database of the institute and all these databases of various hospitals, research institutes and pharmacies are linked using a blockchain.



ABOUT

To protect and promote the work of doctors and scientists, so that the patient healthcare information collected by them is not lost on the vast platform such as internet, we aim to bridge the stated gap and store them in a safe and secure way through blockchain technology. All this is done keeping patient privacy as the prime focus.
